# CHANNEL_STRATEGY

## Priority Channels (MVP)
1. SEO (high intent demand capture)
2. Email lifecycle (activation + review completion)
3. Direct sales outreach (provider-side activation)

## Channel Hypotheses
- SEO pages by service + city will drive lowest blended CAC for demand intent.
- Email nudges at T+2h and T+24h after service completion increase verified reviews.
- Sales-led onboarding scripts reduce provider time-to-first-hire.

## CAC / Efficiency Targets
- SEO: CAC <= ARS 9,000 equivalent.
- Email: CAC <= ARS 4,500 equivalent.
- Sales assist: CAC <= ARS 12,000 equivalent.

## Attribution Model
- Last non-direct click for weekly reporting.
- Assisted conversion flag for email and sales touches.
- Weekly reconciliation against hire completion events.
