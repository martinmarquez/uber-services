# COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS

## Competitive Set
- Informal marketplaces/social groups.
- Classifieds with low trust controls.
- Service directories without verified reputation loops.

## Paperclip Differentiation
- Verified rating eligibility.
- Moderation + appeal workflow.
- Trust-first GTM that links growth to integrity metrics.

## Weaknesses To Monitor
- Potential onboarding friction for first-time providers.
- Need to sustain sufficient demand density by city/category.
