# BRAND_VOICE

Paperclip Services is the trusted way to hire local services in Argentina with verified reputation and transparent decision support.

1. Trust by design: verified reviews and moderation.
2. Speed with clarity: fast matching without hidden risk.
3. Better outcomes for providers: reputation and repeat demand.

## Tone
- Direct, plain Spanish.
- Specific claims, no inflated promises.
- Security and trust language before growth claims.
